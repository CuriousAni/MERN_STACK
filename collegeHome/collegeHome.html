NMIET (Nutan Maharashtra Institute of Engineering and Technology) was founded in 2008. The All India Council for Technical Education (AICTE) in New Delhi and the Maharashtra Government's Directorate of Technical Education (DTE) have both recognized the institute. It is a part of Pune's Savitribai Phule Pune University (SPPU). There are four undergraduate courses offered by the institute. Computer Engineering, Information Technology, Mechanical Engineering, Electronics and Telecommunication Engineerings

Courses Offered

  • BE in Computer Science
  • BE in Information Technology
  • BE in Electronics and Telecommunication
  • BE in Mechanical Engineering
